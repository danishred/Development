Password Managers

What are Password Managers ?

Password Managers are softwares which are becoming essential in this day and age where protection of your digital life (Site Passwords, Bank and Credit card details e.t.c) is of the essence.

How do Password Managers work ?

They generate Random strong Passwords for every site ensuring no two sites have the same passwords and provide a secure encrypted vault to store them. This vault is password protected usina a single Master Password usually but sometimes secure physical keys can also be utilized.

As Password Managers, I personally use Keepass for offline purposes and Bitwarden for cloud storing.
